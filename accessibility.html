Accessibility Statement

iMakeCarKeys is committed to making this website usable for everyone, including people who rely on assistive technology. I want finding a locksmith to be easy no matter how you browse.

Standard followed

This site is designed and tested to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA — the standard referenced by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) for digital accessibility.

Built-in features

  • A "skip to content" link for keyboard and screen-reader users.
  • Full keyboard operability — navigation, menus, photo gallery, and FAQ all work without a mouse.
  • Visible focus outlines so you always know where you are on the page.
  • Descriptive alt text on every image.
  • Semantic structure with correct headings and landmark regions for screen readers.
  • Color contrast that meets or exceeds the WCAG 2.1 AA minimums for all text and controls.
  • Respect for the OS "reduce motion" setting — animations are suppressed when requested.
  • A layout that reflows cleanly on phones, tablets, and when text is enlarged up to 200%.

Accessibility widget

The yellow button in the lower-left corner of every page opens a panel where you can adjust the experience to suit your needs — including larger text (two levels), high contrast, highlighted links, a more readable font, paused motion, and a larger cursor. Your choices are saved on your device and apply across all pages.

Testing

The site is tested with axe-core (WCAG 2.1 A + AA rule set) on desktop and mobile, and is manually checked with keyboard navigation and VoiceOver on macOS.

Ongoing commitment

Accessibility is never finished. I review the site as content changes and welcome any reports of something that's difficult to use.

Need help or found a problem?

If any part of this site is hard to access, or you need information in a different format, please reach out and I'll help directly.

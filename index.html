Questions? I've got answers.

Good to know before you call.

Can you replace my key if I lost the original?

Absolutely — just send your VIN, location, and proof of ownership, and I'll handle the rest.

What brands do you work with?

Most makes and models — domestic, import, and luxury. If you're not sure, call with your year, make, and model and I'll confirm on the spot.

How long does it take to make a car key?

Most keys and fobs are cut and programmed in 20–45 minutes on-site, depending on the vehicle and security system.

Are your fobs original or aftermarket?

I use high-quality OEM and OEM-equivalent fobs that are programmed to factory spec — so they work exactly like the key that came with your car.

Can you make smart keys and push-to-start remotes?

Yes — I specialize in modern vehicles and advanced key systems, including proximity smart keys.

Do I need to tow my car to get a new key or fob?

No. That's the whole point — I come to wherever your car is. No towing, no dealership trip.

Can you program keys or fobs I bought online?

Often, yes — as long as the part is the correct one for your vehicle. Call first and I'll tell you whether your fob will work before you book.

Can you unlock my trunk if my keys are inside?

Yes. Trunk lockouts are routine — I can get you back in quickly without damaging the vehicle.
